Lasagna Recipe

Description:

A deliciously easy cheesy dinner meal

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves Garlic, chopped
  • 800g Beef Mince
  • 1 can diced Tomatoes
  • 2 Cups of shredded Cheese

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in ground beef; cook until no longer pink. Drain excess grease.
  2. Stir in enchilada sauce and diced tomatoes into the pot. Simmer until flavors combine, about 20 minutes. Remove from heat
  3. Preheat oven to 190 degrees C
  4. Mix cottage cheese, egg, and cumin together in a small bowl
  5. Spread 1/3 of the beef sauce in the bottom of baking dish. Cover with corn tortillas. Spread 1/2 cottage cheese mix on top. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top. Repeat layers once more with remaining beef sauce and cheese.
  6. Spray a large piece of aluminium foil with cooking spray; cover baking dish with foil
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly, about 30 minutes. Remove foil and continue baking until top is brown, about 15 mins. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
